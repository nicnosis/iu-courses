J362 - Multimedia Storytelling

This page will host the course content, links, and other materials for MSCH-J362, Multimedia Storytelling.

Week 1

  • Read - The syllabus
  • Complete - Illustrator tutorial - Logo design

Week 2

  • Watch - TED Talk - My Life in Typefaces
  • Read - Thinking With Type - Sections 'LETTER', 'TEXT', and 'GRID'
  • Practice - Creating and using grids in Illustrator. Use this file.
  • Familiarize yourself with - The Lorem Ipsum.
  • Understand - The Golden Rectangle.

Week 3

  • Learn - The brush tool and the blob brush tool in Illustrator
  • Ensure that you know how to create grids/guides in Illustrator.
  • Watch - Powers of Ten.

Week 4

  • Create - a Pinterest account. Use it to gather:
    • Color schemes - Analogous, complementary, triadic.
    • Patterns and symmetry - Use of grids. Tiled patterns. Radial and linear symmetry.
    • Type - Novel use of fonts and text, particularly with respect to arrangement and composition.
    • Imagery that combines composition, color, and type. - Album art, gig posters, and flatstock
